About Us

We are StudentX, where students can buy, sell, and rent anything useful. Do you need a new fan, desk lamp, storage cubes and the likes? Good News, you donâ€™t have to spend a lot to buy new ones. Just join StudentX and buy used ones from your college peers. It is safer, cheaper, and more convenient!

Why Us

StudentX is developed in by five Rutgers University-New Brunswick class of 2024 students as part of our senior capstone project: Peter Chen, Nikhil Agarwal, Ishaan Keswani, Long Phan and Mulugeta Akalu. We thought students have a lot of items to get rid of that can be of much use to other students. We foresee StudentX expanding beyond Rutgers-New Brunswick to host other large universities and colleges.

Security


All sellers and shoppers on the platform will be required to use their college email address to signup, making it safer than other similar sites.

Community


We believe that for a large university like Rutgers, there will be a large selection of items for those that are looking for cheaper and more convenient shopping experience.

24/7


Our team is always ready to help you with any questions or concerns you may have. Feel free to reach out to us with any questions or concerns.

Pay How You Want


Pay in any way you want. As long as the seller agrees to accept that form of payment, then you can use it! We want to make it as easy as possible for you to buy and sell items.
